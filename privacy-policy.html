1. Introduction

Welcome to TikTok India's Privacy Policy. This policy describes how TikTok India ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our application and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio, language preferences
  • User Content: Videos, comments, messages, and other content you create
  • Communication Preferences: Marketing and notification settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, viewing history, engagement metrics
  • Location Data: IP address, general location (if permitted)
  • Network Information: Mobile carrier, connection type

3. How We Use Your Data

Service Provision

  • Content personalization
  • Feature optimization
  • User support

Platform Improvement

  • Analytics and research
  • Performance monitoring
  • Feature development
  • Bug fixing

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Data With:

Important: We implement strict data protection agreements with all third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

TikTok India does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. We implement special protections for teens aged 13-17:

  • Default private accounts
  • Restricted direct messaging
  • Limited data collection
  • Enhanced content filters
